- **Pinned Tweet:** The announcement thread (see below).

### 1.2. Content Strategy

- **Posting Frequency:** 3-5 tweets/day, 1 thread/week.
- **Best Times to Post:** 10 AM - 5 PM, Tuesday-Thursday.
- **Content Pillars:**
  - **Progress Updates (40%):** Daily wins, challenges, code snippets, architecture diagrams.

### 2.2. Engagement Strategy

- **Respond to all comments** within 24 hours.
- **Ask questions** in your tweets to encourage replies.
- **Run weekly polls** on feature prioritization or design choices.
- **Host Twitter Spaces** every 2 weeks with other AI builders.
- **Give shoutouts** to people who provide valuable feedback or contribute to the repo.

### 2.3. Visuals are Key

- **Code Snippets:** Use a tool like Carbon (carbon.now.sh) to create beautiful code images.
- **Architecture Diagrams:** Create clean, easy-to-understand diagrams (I can help with this).
- **GIFs/Screen Recordings:** Use a tool like Kap or Giphy Capture to show the agent in action.
- **Progress Bars:** Create simple visuals to show progress towards the 12-week goal.

## Phase 3: Launch & Post-Launch

### 3.1. Pre-Launch Countdown (1 week before)

- **Daily countdown tweets** with sneak peeks of features.
- **Announce launch date** and time.
- **Offer early-bird pricing** or special bonuses for your community.

### 3.2. Launch Day

- **Launch on Product Hunt & Hacker News.**
- **Post a launch thread** telling the full story from idea to launch.
- **Engage with the community** all day, answering questions and thanking supporters.

### 3.3. Post-Launch

- **Share launch metrics** (sign-ups, revenue, etc.).
- **Showcase customer stories** and testimonials.
- **Continue building in public** with updates, new features, and bug fixes.
- **Monetize through your community** with a Discord/Slack group for premium support or direct access.

---

## Key Takeaways from Viral Accounts

- **Authenticity over Perfection:** Share the messy middle, not just the polished end result.
- **Consistency is King:** A consistent posting schedule builds anticipation and trust.
- **Community is Everything:** Your community is your biggest asset. Nurture it.
- **Visuals Amplify Reach:** A good visual can 10x the engagement of a text-only tweet.
- **Storytelling Sells:** People connect with stories, not just features.

By following this SOP, you'll not only build a powerful AGI agent but also a thriving community of supporters, customers, and collaborators. Let's do this!
